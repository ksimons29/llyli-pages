LLYLI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

LLYLI ("Learn the Language You Live in") is a language learning application that helps users learn vocabulary through spaced repetition. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address — Used for account creation and authentication
  • Apple ID — When using Sign in with Apple (tokenized identifier only)
  • User ID — Unique identifier for your account

2. User-Generated Content

  • Vocabulary words — Words you capture and their translations
  • Example sentences — Sentences generated or provided for learning context
  • Review history — Your answers and progress on vocabulary reviews
  • Learning preferences — Native and target language selections

3. Usage Data

  • Session data — Review session timestamps and duration
  • Progress metrics — Mastery levels, consecutive correct answers
  • FSRS parameters — Spaced repetition algorithm data (difficulty, stability)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Use — App Functionality

  • Spaced Repetition Learning — FSRS algorithm schedules reviews based on your performance
  • Multi-Device Sync — Your vocabulary and progress sync across your devices
  • Personalized Experience — Content adapts to your mastery level

Translation & Content Generation

  • OpenAI API — Word translations, example sentences, and multiple-choice distractors are generated via OpenAI's API through our secure Edge Functions
  • Language Processing — AI determines word categories and difficulty levels

Analytics (Optional)

  • Usage Patterns — Anonymous analytics to improve app experience
  • Crash Reports — Error logs to fix bugs (no personal data)

Data Storage & Security

Supabase Backend

  • All user data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data is encrypted at rest
  • Database access restricted via Row-Level Security policies

Local Device Storage

  • SwiftData stores cached vocabulary and sentences locally for offline use
  • Local data is protected by iOS device encryption
  • Local data is deleted when you delete the app

Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Authentication & data storage Email, user ID, vocabulary data
OpenAI Translation & sentence generation Individual words & language pairs only (no personal data)
Apple Sign in with Apple Tokenized user identifier only

Third-party privacy policies: Supabase, OpenAI, Apple.

Your Rights

Access Your Data

You can view all your vocabulary and progress in the app's Notebook section.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your account at any time:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings tab
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your vocabulary, review history, and progress will be permanently deleted from our servers and your device.

Export Your Data

Contact us via WhatsApp to request a copy of your data in JSON format.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts — Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts — All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Inactive Accounts — Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be archived

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure — Delete your data (via in-app deletion)
  • Right to Data Portability — Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp.

Children's Privacy

LLYLI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us immediately via WhatsApp.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance — Providing the language learning service
  • Legitimate Interest — Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Consent — Analytics and optional features (you can opt out)
