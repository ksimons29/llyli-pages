2. What LLYLI Does

LLYLI helps you learn the language of the country you live in. Capture words from your daily life, and the app turns them into lasting vocabulary with science-backed spaced repetition and real sentence context.

The app currently supports English speakers learning Portuguese (Portugal), Dutch, or Swedish. Review sessions work fully offline.

Free Tier

  • Capture up to 30 words from your real life (onboarding starter words don't count toward this limit)
  • Unlimited review sessions with four exercise types
  • Full access to spaced repetition, progress tracking, and gamification

LLYLI Pro (Subscription)

  • Unlimited word captures
  • Everything in the Free Tier
