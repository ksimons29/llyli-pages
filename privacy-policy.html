LLYLI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

The Short Version

No ads. No tracking. No selling your data. We collect only what's needed to make LLYLI work. Your words belong to you. You can delete everything at any time.

Introduction

LLYLI ("Learn the Language You Live In") helps you learn the language of the country you live in. You capture words from daily life, and the app turns them into lasting vocabulary with spaced repetition and real sentence context.

This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it. We keep it straightforward.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address -- for account creation and authentication
  • Apple ID -- when using Sign in with Apple (tokenized identifier only)
  • User ID -- a unique identifier for your account

2. Your Vocabulary

  • Captured words -- words you add and their translations
  • Practice sentences -- sentences generated for learning context
  • Review history -- your answers and progress on vocabulary reviews
  • Language preferences -- your native and target language selections

3. Usage Data

  • Session data -- review session timestamps and duration
  • Progress metrics -- mastery levels, streaks, consecutive correct answers
  • Learning algorithm data -- how well you know each word and when to review it next
  • Subscription events -- if you subscribe, we record subscription-related events (e.g. trial start, purchase, expiry) for service management

4. Notification Preferences

  • Notification settings -- if you enable review reminders, we store your preference locally on your device

How We Use Your Information

Making the App Work

  • Spaced repetition -- the app schedules reviews based on how well you know each word, so you study less and remember more
  • Multi-device sync -- your vocabulary and progress sync across your devices
  • Personalized exercises -- content adapts to your mastery level. Easy words get harder sentences. Difficult words come back sooner.

Translation and Content

  • OpenAI API -- word translations, example sentences, and quiz options are generated via OpenAI's API through our secure backend. Only individual words and language pairs are sent. No personal data.
  • Language processing -- AI determines word categories and difficulty levels

Analytics (Minimal)

  • Usage patterns -- anonymous, aggregated data to improve the app
  • Error logs -- crash reports to fix bugs (no personal data included)

Data Storage and Security

Cloud (Supabase)

  • All user data stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Database access restricted via Row-Level Security -- you can only see your own data

Your Device

  • Vocabulary and sentences are cached locally for offline use
  • Protected by iOS device encryption
  • Review sessions work 100% offline -- no data leaves your phone
  • Local data is deleted when you delete the app

Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Authentication and data storage Email, user ID, vocabulary data
OpenAI Translation and sentence generation Individual words and language pairs only (no personal data)
Apple Sign in with Apple, subscriptions Tokenized user identifier, subscription status

Third-party privacy policies: Supabase, OpenAI, Apple.

Your Rights

Your data is yours. Here's what you can do with it.

See Your Data

Everything you've captured and learned is visible in the app's Notebook and Progress tabs.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your account at any time:

  1. Tap the info button (top-right on any tab)
  2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion
This is permanent and cannot be undone. All your vocabulary, review history, and progress will be permanently deleted from our servers and your device within 30 days.

Export Your Data

Contact us via WhatsApp or email to request a copy of your data in JSON format.

EU Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access -- request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification -- correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure -- delete your data (via in-app deletion)
  • Right to Data Portability -- export your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object -- object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance -- providing the language learning service you signed up for
  • Legitimate Interest -- improving app functionality and fixing bugs
  • Consent -- optional features like notifications (you can opt out anytime)

Data Retention

  • Active accounts -- data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts -- all data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Inactive accounts -- accounts inactive for 2+ years may be archived

Children's Privacy

LLYLI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us immediately.
